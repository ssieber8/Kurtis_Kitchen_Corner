Simple chicken ramen

For 4

3 1 4 2 l 200g 125 ml 50 ml 20 g 2
Spring onions Leek Chicken breast Chicken broth Ramen noodles Light soy sauce Mirin Spice mix Eggs

Step 1

First, chop the spring onions into small pieces. Then wash the leeks well and also cut them into small pieces. Put both ingredients in a bowl and set aside.

Step 2

Wash the chicken breast and cut into small slices. Fry the chicken well in a pan. In the meantime, you can prepare the chicken broth. Put in 2 liters of water into a pot and add the chicken broth cubes. Heat the water. When the chicken is well browned, put the pieces aside on a plate. Set the broth aside as well.

Step 3

Now cook the ramen in the pot with the chicken broth for about 4 minutes. While it is cooking, add the chicken, the spring onions, and the chopped leeks. Then you can add the soy sauce and mirin as well as the spice mixture. Finally, before serving, cut the hard egg in two and place it on top of the ramen.
